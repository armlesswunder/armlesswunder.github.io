The Isle of Centiel

The isle of Centiel is a dangerous, yet beautiful Island which holds a dark secret deep beneath the surface. Lord Ondor, a harsh overlord once occupied the island with his orcish legion. It is said that he was buried with his vast treasure horde deep beneath the earth, hidden away in a huge and complex labyrinth. Can you gather the fabled Jewels of Centiel and open the way to the long forgotten tomb of Lord Ondor? Friendship, betrayal, danger, and untold riches will meet you on your journey.

Mariposa Military Base

This is a small quest allowing you to travel to Mariposa military base, the source of the Master's super mutant army (from Fallout 1).
