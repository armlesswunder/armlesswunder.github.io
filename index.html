Animal Crossing Catalog

Use this guide to keep track of what items you have in Animal Crossing for Gamecube, Wild World, City Folk, New Leaf, and New Horizons. Quickly search for items by name or where the item is from and see important details like price, style, unorderable, etc... Fish, Insects, and Seafood can be searched for by the month they are available: never miss that valuable catch again! Special thanks to Nintendo, Liquify, and everyone who contributed to the data sheet available at Nookazon for making all this possible!
